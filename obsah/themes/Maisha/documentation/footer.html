Footer

This theme offers widgetized footer.

3.1 Footer

Navigate to the Appearance > Widgets to find your available theme widgets.

You can have one column, two columns, three columns, four columns or five columns widgetized footer. If you drag and drop one widget inside the "Footer Widget Area" you'll have one column footer area. If you drag and drop two widgets inside "Footer Widget Area" you'll have two columns footer area and if you upload three widgets you'll have three columns footer area.
